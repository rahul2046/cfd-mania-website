Meet the Founder

Reddy Rahul Saddala

CFD & Aerodynamics Enthusiast | NITK Surathkal

Rahul is a passionate mechanical engineering student from NITK Surathkal with a deep-rooted expertise in Computational Fluid Dynamics and Aerodynamics. His journey is marked by hands-on experience, from contributing to the aerodynamics subsystem for a Formula Student Electric Vehicle to conducting research on blended wing body aircraft at the prestigious National Aerospace Laboratories.

CFD Mania was born from his vision to bridge the gap between complex academic theory and practical industry application, making high-level CFD knowledge accessible to students and professionals alike.
